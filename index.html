FAQ

Key questions answered clearly

Does Postgame Vision require a subscription?

No. The app is fully usable without a subscription.

Can I import from Photos and Files?

Yes. Both import paths are supported.

Can I tag by player?

Yes. Tags and notes can be assigned to specific players or team-level events.

Can I export stats?

Yes. Team and player reports are exportable as CSV and PDF.

Can I export clips to Camera Roll?

Yes, when Photos permission is granted.

Does it work offline?

Yes for the core workflow. Import, export, and local project data work on-device.

Can I review multiple videos for one game?

Yes. One project can include multiple clips, such as one file per quarter.

Privacy Policy

Data Stored Locally
